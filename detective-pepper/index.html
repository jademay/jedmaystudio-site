Chapter 1 — The Young Detective

Detective Pepper starts at home with household mysteries. Think: missing items, playful chaos, and suspicious suspects.

What you'll do

  • Read clues and eliminate possibilities
  • Fill the logic grid with ticks and crosses
  • Submit your solution, then pick the final accusation
  • Unlock the next case and continue the story

Cosy content note

The tone is playful and light. Some mysteries use classic "whodunnit" language, but the vibe is cosy and cartoonish rather than graphic.
