About Detective Pepper

Detective Pepper is a cosy logic-grid puzzle game starring a tiny tuxedo cat detective. Players read clues, eliminate possibilities, and decide who the culprit is in each case.

The tone is light and playful, with cartoon mysteries and satisfying deductions rather than graphic content.
